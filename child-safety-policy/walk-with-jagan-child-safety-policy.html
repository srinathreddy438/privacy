Child Safety and Protection Policy

Overview

Walk With Jagan Anna is committed to protecting children from all forms of sexual exploitation and abuse. We prohibit any child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any content that sexualizes or exploits minors.

Content moderation & prevention

  • We monitor user-generated content and remove any content that violates our policies.
  • We use automated filters and manual review where applicable to detect and remove harmful content.
  • Accounts that post prohibited content will be suspended and may be reported to the appropriate authorities.

Reporting

If you encounter content or behaviour that may harm a child, please report it immediately to: svappzone@gmail.com. Please include screenshots, usernames, timestamps, and any other relevant details to help our review.

Law & cooperation

We comply with all applicable child protection laws and will cooperate with law enforcement and relevant authorities as required.

Review

This policy is reviewed at least annually and updated as needed.
